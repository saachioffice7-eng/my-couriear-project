# Quick Start Guide - SAACHI Courier Management System

## 🚀 Get Started in 5 Minutes!

---

## Part 1: Courier Booking (Smart Features)

### Step 1: Add a Parcel with Smart Auto-Fill

1. **Go to Parcels page**
2. **Click "Add Parcel"**
3. **Enter PIN Code**: `400064`
   - ✨ City, State, Country auto-fill!
4. **Select Courier**: `Maruti`
   - ✨ Base rate auto-loads!
5. **Enter Weight**: `1200`
   - ✨ Amount auto-calculates!
6. **Fill remaining fields** (name, phone, address)
7. **Click "Create Parcel"**

**Result**: Parcel created with minimal typing! 🎉

## Part 2: Expense Management (New System)

### Step 1: Add Your First Expense

1. **Go to Expenses page**
2. **Add Expense tab** (default)
3. **Fill the form**:
   - Date: Today (auto-filled)
   - Person Name: `John Doe`
   - Expense Type: `Conveyance`
   - Payment Mode: `Cash`
   - Amount: `500`
   - Title: `Taxi to office`
   - Description: `Morning commute`
4. **Upload slip** (optional)
5. **Click "Save Expense"**

**Result**: 
- ✅ Expense saved
- ✅ Cash balance decreased by ₹500
- ✅ Entry appears in Expense Sheet
- ✅ Entry appears in CA Ledger

---

### Step 2: View Expense Sheet

1. **Click "Expense Sheet" tab**
2. **See your expense** in the table
3. **Try filters**:
   - Search by name
   - Filter by type
   - Select date range
4. **Export**:
   - Click "Excel" to download
   - Or "PDF" for report
   - Or "Print" for hard copy

---

### Step 3: View CA Ledger

1. **Click "CA Ledger" tab**
2. **See accounting entries**:
   - Debit: ₹500 (your expense)
   - Credit: -
3. **Check totals**:
   - Total Debit
   - Total Credit
   - Net Balance
4. **Export for CA/Auditor**

---

### Step 4: View Analytics

1. **Click "Analytics" tab**
2. **See dashboard**:
   - Wallet balances
   - Today's expense
   - Monthly expense
3. **View charts**:
   - Category breakdown (Pie Chart)
   - Monthly trend (Bar Chart)
   - Payment mode split
4. **Analyze spending patterns**

---

### Step 5: Generate Reports

1. **Click "Reports" tab**
2. **Select report type**:
   - Category Wise
   - Monthly Summary
   - Person Wise
3. **Apply filters** (optional)
4. **View report** with percentages
5. **Export** (Excel/PDF/Print)

## Part 3: Bank Withdrawal Example

### How to Transfer Money from Bank to Cash

1. **Go to Expenses → Add Expense**
2. **Fill form**:
   - Expense Type: `Bank Withdrawal`
   - Amount: `5000`
   - Title: `Cash for office expenses`
3. **Click "Save Expense"**

**Result**:
- ✅ Bank Balance: -₹5000
- ✅ Cash Balance: +₹5000
- ✅ Two ledger entries created automatically

## Part 5: Configure Rates (For Auto-Calculation)

### Set Up Courier Rates

1. **Go to Rates page**
2. **Click "Add Rate"**
3. **Fill form**:
   - Courier Company: `Maruti`
   - City: `Mumbai`
   - Base Rate: `50`
4. **Click "Create"**

**Result**: Now when you add parcels for Maruti → Mumbai, amount auto-calculates!
